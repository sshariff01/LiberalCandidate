Community Involvement

  • Youth coordinator and mentor of a local Community Services Group for the past 13 years. Formed a sports club, organized festivals and helped with fundraising events.
  • Assisted former City of Mississauga, Ward Six Councillor to get an approval for a Cricket ground on White Clover Way. An initiative for youth’s healthy activities.
  • For the past 13 years, has organized an Annual Canada Day BBQ celebration in the neighbourhood where he resides.
  • Has helped fundraise for local Hospitals, Food Banks and various other community organizations
 

Political Involvement

  • Recently served as the Executive Vice President on the board of Mississauga-Erindale Federal Liberal Association (FLA).
  • 2002-2012 Served on the board of Mississauga-Erindale FLA as:
    • Chair of the Candidate Search Committee in 2006
    • Secretary, Treasurer and Director on the Mississauga-Erindale FLA
    • Director of Multiculturalism on the board of Mississauga-Erindale Provincial Liberal Association
    • Elected Delegate for 2006 Leadership Convention
    • Riding Chair for Mississauga-Erindale during Justin Trudeau’s Leadership Campaign
    • Participated in Municipal Elections of Mississauga and ran for Public School Board Trustee in 2006
    • Assisted local MPs and MPPs in their Nominations and Election Campaigns
